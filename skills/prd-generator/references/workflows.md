# PRD Generation Workflow

Follow these steps to generate and refine a PRD.

## Step 1: Context Building
- Interview stakeholders or review existing notes.
- Use the checklist in `requirements.md` to identify gaps in information.

## Step 2: Outline Selection
- Evaluate the feature type (Standard, Technical, or UX).
- Map the gathered requirements to the relevant sections of the chosen outline from `outlines.md`.

## Step 3: Drafting
- Use `templates/prd-template.md` as the starting point.
- Synthesize requirements into clear, concise sections.
- Prioritize user stories and functional requirements (e.g., P0, P1, P2).

## Step 4: Review & Refinement
- Review for clarity and technical feasibility.
- Add "Edge Cases" and "Out of Scope" sections to prevent scope creep.
- Finalize success metrics.

## Step 5: Stakeholder Alignment
- Present the draft for feedback.
- Iterate based on technical and design reviews.
