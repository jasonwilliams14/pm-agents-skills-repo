# Requirements Gathering Checklist

Before generating a PRD, ensure the following information is gathered:

## 1. Problem Statement
- What is the specific pain point we are solving?
- Who is affected by this problem?
- What is the impact of *not* solving it?

## 2. Target Audience
- Who are the primary users?
- Are there secondary stakeholders (e.g., admins, support)?

## 3. Core Value Proposition
- What is the primary benefit of this feature?
- How does it align with the product's overall mission?

## 4. Key Constraints
- Technical limitations?
- Timeframe or budget constraints?
- Legal or compliance requirements?

## 5. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- How will we know if this is successful?
- What data points will we track?
