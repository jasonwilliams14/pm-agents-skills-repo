# PRD & Summary Templates

## PRD Structure
1. **Title & Stakeholders**
2. **Executive Summary** (Value/Risk)
3. **Problem Statement** (JTBD)
4. **Proposed Solution** (POC-First focus)
5. **Success Metrics** (OTEL-based)
6. **RICE Score**
7. **Differentiation** (Value Prop vs. Competitors)

## Stakeholder Lens Summaries
When providing summaries, always provide three versions:

### 1. Executive
- **Focus:** Value, Risk, Strategic Alignment.
- **Goal:** Quick decision-making.

### 2. Engineering
- **Focus:** Logic, Constraints, Implementation details.
- **Goal:** Technical clarity.

### 3. Product
- **Focus:** Outcome, UX, Customer impact.
- **Goal:** Alignment on user value.
